Frequently Asked Questions

How do I import my Tesla dashcam clips?
Connect your Tesla USB drive to your iPhone/iPad using a USB-C adapter or Lightning to USB adapter. Then tap "Import" in the app and select "Intelligent Scan" to automatically find all your clips.
Why are some cameras missing in my clips?
This usually means those camera angles weren't recording at the time. Older Tesla vehicles (HW3) have 4 cameras, while newer vehicles (HW4) have 6 cameras including pillar cameras.
Why is the telemetry data missing?
As of the December 2025 release, telemetry (speed, GPS, etc.) is supported for Sentry, Dashcam, Saved Clips, and Recents when source metadata is present. If telemetry is missing, re-import the original files with Intelligent Scan.
How do I export in higher quality?
Tap the Export button and select your desired quality level. Higher resolutions like 4K require more processing time and storage space.
What is GuardReel Pro?
GuardReel Pro is an optional yearly subscription ($2.99/year) that removes all advertisements, unlocks all export qualities, and lets you customize the watermark. All core features are available in the free version — Pro provides an ad-free experience with full-resolution exports and custom watermarks.
How do I cancel my Pro subscription?
Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad, tap your name at the top, then tap Subscriptions. Find GuardReel Pro and tap Cancel Subscription. Your Pro benefits continue until the end of the current billing period.
How do I restore my Pro purchase on a new device?
In the app, go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.
Can I remove the watermark?
The watermark is included in all exported videos. GuardReel Pro subscribers can customize the watermark text.
Is my video data uploaded anywhere?
No. All video processing happens entirely on your device. Your videos never leave your phone or tablet unless you choose to share them yourself.
How do I change the camera in a layout slot?
Tap and hold on any camera view in the preview to open the camera selection menu. You can assign any available camera to any slot.

Your Data & Privacy Rights

🔒 What Data Does GuardReel Have About Me?

GuardReel does not maintain user accounts and does not store personally identifiable information on our servers. Here's exactly what exists:

  • Your videos: 100% on your device. We never upload, access, or store them.
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage patterns (screens viewed, features used) via Firebase. Cannot be linked to you personally.
  • Crash reports: Device model + stack traces via Crashlytics. No personal identifiers. Auto-deleted after 90 days.
  • Advertising: If you allowed tracking, Google AdMob may have data tied to your device's IDFA. This is controlled by Apple, not us.
  • Purchases: Handled entirely by Apple. We only see whether you have an active subscription — no payment details.

🗑️ Delete My Data

Since we don't store personal data on our servers, here's how to remove everything:

  1. Delete local app data: Delete GuardReel from your device (Settings → General → iPhone Storage → GuardReel → Delete App)
  2. Reset your advertising ID: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → toggle off GuardReel, or reset your IDFA in Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising
  3. Revoke ad consent (EU users): Before deleting, open GuardReel → Settings → Ad Privacy Settings → withdraw consent
  4. Request analytics deletion: Email us at [email protected] — we'll confirm deletion of any anonymized analytics data that Firebase can associate with your device

⚙️ Control Your Privacy Settings

You can control what data is collected at any time:

  • Stop ad tracking: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable for GuardReel
  • Remove all ads: Subscribe to GuardReel Pro ($2.99/yr) — no advertising data is collected for Pro users
  • Manage GDPR consent: In-app Settings → Ad Privacy Settings (available to EU users)
  • Google ad preferences: adssettings.google.com

📬 Submit a Privacy Request

If you'd like to exercise your data rights under GDPR, CCPA, UK GDPR, LGPD, PIPEDA, or any other applicable privacy law, email us. Include "Privacy Request" in the subject line and tell us what you need (access, deletion, correction, portability, or opt-out). We respond within 30 days.

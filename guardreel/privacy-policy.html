Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 27, 2026

GuardReel ("we", "our", or "us") operates the GuardReel mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

1. Video Data (Local Only)

  • Tesla Dashcam Videos: The App processes Tesla dashcam videos stored on your device or connected USB drives
  • Embedded Telemetry: Speed, GPS coordinates, steering angle, and other vehicle data embedded in Tesla video files
  • Storage: All video processing occurs locally on your device. We do not upload, transmit, or store your videos on any servers

2. Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data to improve the App:

  • App opens and screen views
  • Feature usage (exports, layouts used)
  • Device type and iOS version
  • Crash reports and performance data

This data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify you personally.

3. Advertising Data

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements:

  • With Tracking Permission: If you allow tracking via App Tracking Transparency (ATT), ads may be personalized based on your interests
  • Without Tracking Permission: You will see contextual (non-personalized) ads
  • AdMob may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data per Google's Privacy Policy

4. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

iOS requires us to ask permission before tracking your activity across other companies' apps and websites. You can:

  • Allow Tracking: Receive personalized ads
  • Deny Tracking: Receive non-personalized ads (the App functions identically either way)
  • Change your choice anytime in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking

How We Use Your Information

  • Video Processing: To edit, combine, and export your Tesla dashcam footage
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with the App and improve features
  • Advertising: To display ads and support free access to the App
  • Crash Reporting: To identify and fix bugs

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Google Firebase: Analytics and crash reporting
  • Google AdMob: Advertising services
  • Apple: App Store analytics (if enabled in your Apple ID settings)

Data Retention

  • Video Data: Never leaves your device; deleted when you remove videos
  • Analytics Data: Retained by Firebase per their data retention policies
  • Ad Data: Managed by Google per their privacy policy

Your Rights

You can:

  • Deny tracking permission via ATT prompt or iOS Settings
  • Request deletion of analytics data by contacting us
  • Opt out of personalized ads via iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures. All video processing occurs locally on your device, minimizing data exposure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

For privacy questions or data requests:

  • Email: privacy@guardreel.app

This privacy policy is designed to comply with Apple App Store requirements, GDPR, and CCPA.

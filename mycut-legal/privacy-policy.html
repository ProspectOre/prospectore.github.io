1. Introduction

MyCut is operated by Prospectore LLC, an Oregon limited liability company ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the MyCut mobile application (the "App").

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the App.

This policy applies to all users worldwide. Where specific jurisdictions grant additional rights (such as the EU/EEA, UK, California, or Brazil), those rights are described in Section 9.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Deal & Financial Data (Stored Locally)

You enter deal information including vehicle details, sale prices, costs, customer names, and pay plan settings. This data is:

  • Stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
  • Optionally synced to your private iCloud account (if iCloud is enabled on your device)
  • Never sent to our servers
  • Never shared with third parties

2.2 Analytics Data (Collected Automatically)

We automatically collect certain information when you use the App to help us understand usage patterns and improve our services:

Data TypeExamplesPurpose
Usage DataFeatures used, screens viewed, button tapsImprove app functionality
Device InformationDevice model, iOS version, screen sizeOptimize compatibility
Performance DataLoad times, memory usagePerformance optimization
Crash DataCrash logs, error reports, stack tracesBug fixes and stability
Approximate LocationCity or region inferred from IP address (not GPS)Serve regionally relevant ads

This data is collected in an anonymized or pseudonymized form and cannot be used to directly identify you personally.

2.3 Purchase Information

If you subscribe to MyCut Pro, Apple processes and stores all payment and subscription information. We receive only:

  • Subscription status: Whether you have an active Pro subscription (to remove ads)
  • Transaction verification: Cryptographic receipt data to validate your purchase

We do not receive or store your payment method, billing address, or Apple ID. All billing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store.

2.4 Advertising Identifiers

If you grant permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt, we may collect:

  • Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA): Used to deliver personalized advertisements and measure ad effectiveness

If you deny tracking permission, we do not collect your IDFA and you will only see contextual (non-personalized) advertisements.

2.5 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or contact information (unless you contact us directly)
  • Your Apple ID or account credentials
  • Your deal amounts, vehicle details, customer names, or commission figures
  • Your precise real-time location via GPS, Wi-Fi, or cellular signals
  • Contacts, calendar, photos, or other personal data
  • Biometric data (Face ID / Touch ID are handled by iOS, not the App)

2.6 Demo Mode

If you use "Explore Demo" to preview the app:

  • Sample data is generated for demonstration purposes only
  • Demo data is automatically deleted when the app closes or restarts
  • Nothing from demo mode is stored permanently or synced
  • Ads may be shown during demo mode

4. Advertising & Consent

4.1 Ad-Supported Model

MyCut is a free app supported by advertisements provided by Google AdMob and Meta Audience Network. Ads help us keep the App free while continuing to develop new features. You can remove all advertisements by subscribing to MyCut Pro ($0.99/month). Pro subscribers do not see any ads, and no advertising data is collected for Pro users.

4.2 Personalized vs. Non-Personalized Ads

The type of ads you see depends on your App Tracking Transparency choice:

Your ChoiceAd Experience
Allow TrackingPersonalized ads based on your interests and activity across apps. Ad partners may use your IDFA to measure ad effectiveness.
Ask App Not to TrackContextual ads that are not based on your personal data. You still see ads, but they are generic and not tailored to you.

4.3 Consent Management (EEA/UK)

For users in the European Economic Area and United Kingdom, we use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to present a consent dialog that complies with the IAB Transparency and Consent Framework (TCF v2). You can update your consent preferences at any time through the consent dialog presented in the app, or by resetting your advertising identifier in iOS Settings.

4.4 Ad Partner Data Practices

Google AdMob and its mediation partners may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies. AdMob's ad serving infrastructure may contact third-party ad networks, including Meta (Facebook) Audience Network, during the ad auction process:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from AdMob
  • Meta (Facebook) Privacy Policy

4.5 Managing Ad Preferences

You can control personalized advertising through:

  • iOS Settings: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • In-App (EU/UK Users): Consent dialog for GDPR consent management
  • Google Ad Settings: adssettings.google.com
  • Remove Ads Entirely: Subscribe to MyCut Pro in Settings

5.1 Apple iCloud (CloudKit)

If iCloud sync is enabled, your deal and pay plan data syncs across your Apple devices via Apple's CloudKit infrastructure. We cannot access your iCloud data. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

5.2 Apple StoreKit

In-app purchases (MyCut Pro) are processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store payment information. See Apple's Terms.

5.4 Google AdMob & Meta Audience Network

Displays advertisements and may collect advertising identifiers if permitted. AdMob's mediation infrastructure may contact Meta Audience Network during ad auctions. See Section 4 for details.

6. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share anonymized, aggregated data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party services listed in Section 5, which process data on our behalf under their respective privacy policies
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, subpoena, court order, or government request
  • Safety: To protect against fraud, security threats, or to enforce our Terms of Service
  • Business Transfer: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (you would be notified)

Your deal data, commission figures, and customer names are never shared with anyone. They exist only on your device and in your private iCloud account.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • On-Device Storage: Your financial data is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework with iOS sandboxing protections
  • iCloud Encryption: Data synced via CloudKit is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
  • No Server Storage: We do not operate servers that store your personal or financial data, eliminating server-side breach risk
  • Subscription Verification: Purchase validation uses Apple's cryptographic receipt verification

While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but the on-device architecture significantly limits exposure.

9. Your Privacy Rights

9.1 All Users

Regardless of your location, you can:

  • Export your data: Use the CSV export feature in Settings > Data Management
  • Delete your data: Settings > Data Management > Delete All Deals or Delete All Data & Reset App
  • Disable iCloud sync: Toggle off in Settings > Data Management
  • Control ad tracking: iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Remove ads: Subscribe to MyCut Pro in Settings
  • Request data deletion: Contact us at the email below

9.2 European Economic Area & United Kingdom (GDPR / UK GDPR)

If you are in the EEA or UK, you have the following additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing of your personal data
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (CSV export)
  • Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interest, including profiling
  • Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent (e.g., personalized ads), withdraw at any time without affecting prior processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.

Supervisory Authority: You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. A list of EEA authorities is available at edpb.europa.eu. For UK users, contact the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

9.3 California (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act and California Privacy Rights Act:

  • Right to Know: Request what personal information we collect, use, and disclose
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing: We do not sell your personal information. Advertising identifiers shared with ad partners for personalized ads may constitute "sharing" under CPRA — you can opt out via iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights

Categories of personal information collected: Device identifiers (advertising ID, if permitted), usage data, crash data, approximate location (IP-derived). We do not collect sensitive personal information as defined by CPRA.

"Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information": We do not sell personal information. To opt out of sharing for cross-context behavioral advertising, disable tracking in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

9.4 Brazil (LGPD)

If you are in Brazil, you have rights under the Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados including access, correction, deletion, portability, and information about sharing. Contact us at the email below to exercise these rights.

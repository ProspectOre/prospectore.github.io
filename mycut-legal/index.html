Your data stays with you. MyCut stores all deal and commission data on your device and in your private iCloud account. We do not have access to your financial data and cannot view, modify, or delete it remotely. You have full control.

Manage Your Data

You can do all of the following directly from the app — no need to contact us:

Delete Your Data

  1. Open MyCut → Settings → Data Management
  2. Tap "Delete All Deals" to remove all deal records, or "Delete All Data & Reset App" to remove everything and start fresh
  3. If you had iCloud sync enabled, also clear iCloud data: iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → MyCut → Delete Data

Uninstalling MyCut from your device also deletes all locally stored data.

Export Your Data

  1. Open MyCut → Settings → Data Management → Export
  2. A CSV file will be generated containing all your deal records
  3. Share via email, AirDrop, Files, or any other method

CSV is a machine-readable format that satisfies GDPR data portability (Article 20) and CCPA access rights.

Control Ad Tracking

  1. Go to iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  2. Disable tracking for MyCut (or disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track" globally)
  3. MyCut will show only contextual (non-personalized) ads

EU/UK users: You can also update ad consent preferences through the in-app consent dialog powered by Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) with the IAB Transparency and Consent Framework (TCF v2).

Remove ads entirely: Subscribe to MyCut Pro ($0.99/month) in the app's Settings. Pro subscribers see no ads, and in-app ad requests are disabled while Pro is active.

Cancel MyCut Pro

  1. Open iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions
  2. Tap MyCut Pro → Cancel Subscription
  3. You retain Pro benefits until the end of your current billing period

Refund requests must go through Apple: reportaproblem.apple.com

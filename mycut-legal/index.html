Manage your data, understand your rights, and get help with MyCut.

Your data stays with you. MyCut stores all deal and commission data on your device and in your private iCloud account. We do not have access to your financial data and cannot view, modify, or delete it remotely. You have full control.

Manage Your Data

You can do all of the following directly from the app — no need to contact us:

Delete Your Data

  1. Open MyCut → Settings → Data Management
  2. Tap "Delete All Deals" to remove all deal records, or "Delete All Data & Reset App" to remove everything and start fresh
  3. If you had iCloud sync enabled, also clear iCloud data: iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → MyCut → Delete Data

Uninstalling MyCut from your device also deletes all locally stored data.

Export Your Data

  1. Open MyCut → Settings → Data Management → Export
  2. A CSV file will be generated containing all your deal records
  3. Share via email, AirDrop, Files, or any other method

CSV is a machine-readable format that satisfies GDPR data portability (Article 20) and CCPA access rights.

Control Ad Tracking

  1. Go to iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  2. Disable tracking for MyCut (or disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track" globally)
  3. MyCut will show only contextual (non-personalized) ads

EU/UK users: You can also update your ad consent preferences through the consent dialog in the app.

Remove ads entirely: Subscribe to MyCut Pro ($0.99/month) in the app's Settings. Pro subscribers see no ads and no advertising data is collected.

Cancel MyCut Pro

  1. Open iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions
  2. Tap MyCut Pro → Cancel Subscription
  3. You retain Pro benefits until the end of your current billing period

Refund requests must go through Apple: reportaproblem.apple.com

Frequently Asked Questions

What data does MyCut collect?

MyCut collects anonymized analytics only — things like which screens you visit, your device model, and crash reports. This data cannot identify you personally.

Your deal data, customer names, commission figures, and pay plan settings are stored only on your device and in your private iCloud account. We never see this data.

If you allow ad tracking, Google AdMob and Meta Audience Network may collect your advertising identifier to show personalized ads. See Privacy Policy for full details.

Does MyCut sell my data?

No. We do not sell your personal information to anyone. If you allow ad tracking, your device's advertising identifier may be shared with ad partners for personalized ads — which may constitute "sharing" under California's CPRA. You can opt out by disabling tracking in iOS Settings, or by subscribing to MyCut Pro.

Can you delete my data for me?

We cannot access or delete your deal data because it exists only on your device and in your private iCloud account. You can delete it yourself using the steps above.

Anonymized analytics (Firebase) are automatically purged — analytics after 14 months, crash reports after 90 days. This data cannot be linked to you individually.

What happens to my data if I uninstall MyCut?

Uninstalling MyCut deletes all locally stored data from your device. If you had iCloud sync enabled, your data may persist in your iCloud account until you manually delete it (iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → MyCut).

Is my data encrypted?

Yes. Data stored on your device is protected by iOS sandboxing. Data synced via iCloud is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple. We do not operate any servers that store your data.

I'm in the EU/UK — what are my GDPR rights?

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, port, and object to processing of your personal data. Since your financial data is stored on your device, you can exercise most of these rights directly using the self-service tools above. For any request we need to handle, contact us and we'll respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. See Privacy Policy Section 9.2 for details.

I'm in California — what are my CCPA rights?

You have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, opt out of the sale/sharing of personal information, and not be discriminated against for exercising your rights.

We do not sell personal information. To opt out of sharing for ad personalization, disable tracking in iOS Settings. See Privacy Policy Section 9.3 for full details.

Are commission calculations accurate?

MyCut provides estimates only, based on the pay plan settings and deal information you enter. Always verify your actual earnings with your dealership's official records or payroll department. See our Terms of Service Section 5.
